## Description 
> studySpartan is a web application designed to help students find study groups, TAs, or tutors for academic support. 
> Students can connect with tutors or peers to study challenging subjects, focus on learning, or get extra help with assignments. 
>
> The motivation for the app is to make studying more effective and collaborative by matching students with the right support at the right time.
> The goal is to provide a platform where students can easily schedule sessions, join study groups, and give feedback, while tutors can manage their availability and interact with students.

## App Functions

1. Student (the user with the student role):
    1. Create/modify student profile - Students can register and update their personal information, including name, courses, and contact details.
    2. View available tutors or study groups - Students can browse tutors and groups, see their subjects and availability, and view session details.
    3. Book tutoring or study sessions - Students can select a time slot with a tutor based on availability and confirm their booking.
    4. Give feedback - After a session, students can leave ratings and comments to provide feedback for tutors and study groups.

2. Tutor (the user with the tutor role):
    1. Create/modify tutor profile - Tutors can set up and update their profiles with personal info, subjects they teach, and credentials.
    2. Set available times - Tutors can manage their schedule to indicate when they are available for sessions.
    3. View student bookings - Tutors can see which students booked sessions, and manage their schedule accordingly.
    4. Reply to feedback - Tutors can respond to student feedback to clarify issues or acknowledge positive comments.

3. SysAdmin (moderators, presumably us):
    1. Manage user profiles - SysAdmins make sure users' information is correctly displayed and appropriate for the platform.
    2. Moderate messaging - SysAdmins make sure users are using the chatting features appropriately and responding to chat reports made.
    3. General Moderatation - SysAdmins check that inappropriate profiles, chat features, and off-platform wrongdoing that is reported is properly dealt with.
    4. View usage - SysAdmins should be able to check the aspects of the application as though they were "playing any role," to ensure that the application works smoothly.
